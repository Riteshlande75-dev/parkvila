Two Wheeler Parking

Two-wheeler parking refers to designated areas for motorcycles and scooters. These can be on-street or in parking lots, often marked with clear symbols or lines. Parking spaces may be secured with CCTV or attendants, and some may offer locking racks for additional safety

Payment for parking may be hourly, daily, or via subscription, with options for online booking in some places. Regulations may include time limits, and improper parking can lead to fines or towing. Environmental features like electric vehicle charging stations may also be available in certain locations.

Four Wheeler Parking

A four-wheeler parking slot is a designated space where a car can be parked. It is typically rectangular in shape, wide enough to accommodate the length and width of a standard car, and marked with lines to guide proper parking. These slots are found in parking lots, garages, or on the street.
